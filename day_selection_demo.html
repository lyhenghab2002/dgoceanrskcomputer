How to Use

  1. Set Date Range: Use the "From Date" and "To Date" inputs to define your range
  2. View Table: The day table will automatically populate with all days in your range
  3. View Details: Click the "View" button next to any day to see detailed data for that specific day
  4. Export Individual Days: Click the "Export" button to export data for a specific day
  5. Browse Data: Each row shows day number, full date, revenue, and order count
  6. Export Range: Use the PDF or CSV export buttons to download your filtered data
